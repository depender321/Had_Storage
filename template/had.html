HOW IT WORKS




Storage has never been so easy. Just follow
our four simple steps. If you’re a business it’s
just as simple.






1. YOU BOOK



Tell us how much stuff you want to store and how long you want to store for. You can
order cardboard boxes and other packing materials if you need them, or just book for us
to collect your stuff if you’re already packed.




2. WE COLLECT



We’ll come to collect your items on the day that's most convenient for you. Just give your
labelled boxes or other items to your friendly driver, then you can track your stuff online.
Make sure you download our app to help everything go extra smoothly!




3. WE STORE



We carefully store your stuff in a secure CCTV-monitored storage facility. Ready and
waiting for when you need them back.




4. WE DELIVER



When you’re ready, just book the delivery of one, some, or all of your boxes online from
5.95 per box. Oh, and remember an additional charge of 1 per box applies if
delivering to an address greater than 80 miles from your collection location.




WHY USE HAD STORAGE?




LOVESPACE was the perfect solution for me whilst I was
moving in between houses. I simply took delivery of the
boxes, packed them and they were collected on moving
day. When I was settled into my new home, some 8 weeks
later, my boxes were delivered to my new house. I saved
money on expensive storage fees, didn’t need to hire a car
or van and I could organise everything online (usually on the bus to work).
